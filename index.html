Here's a time line of Freddie's life:

  • 1946 - Born as Farrokh Bulsara in Stone Town, Zanzibar
  • 1964 - Moves to London with his family
  • 1964 - Graduates from West Thames College in West London where he studied art
  • 1969 - Tries to make a living from selling second-hand clothes in the Kensington Market in London and working at Heathrow Airport
  • 1970 - Joines giutarist Brian May and drummer Roger Taylor who had previously been in a band called Smile. In the next year they found heir bass player John Deacon and change their band's name to Queen.
  • 1970 - Changes his surmane to Mercury
  • 1973 - Together with the band releases their debut Album called Queen
  • 1975 - Queen releases their first UK nr 1 album called A Night At The Opera with a rock ballad "Bohemian Rhapsody" which is considered as Freddie's opus vitea
  • 1991 - Queen gives their most famous live performance at Live Aid concert held at Wembley Stadium
  • 1985 - Dies at age 45 due to complications from AIDS

"The difference between Freddie and almost all the other rock stars was that he was selling the voice."
- Montserrat Caballé, Spanish operatic soprano who recorded with Freddie album "Barcelona" in 1987
